About Us

Rexkamp Co. Ltd has been in the electrical retail industry for many decades specialising in high quality products and with great knowledge in various electrical components and accessories. Over the years we have partnered with so many industry giants both locally and internationally to provide our customers with a diverse range of products. We are active members of the Ghana Electrical Dealers Association (GEDA) and have been a part of it since its inception, playing a vital role in the industry.
